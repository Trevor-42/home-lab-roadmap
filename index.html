Pro Tips
1
Label every cable end — both ends, with masking tape and a marker or a label maker. You'll thank yourself 6 months later when you need to swap something.
2
Use short patch cables — 1ft and 2ft cables in different colors for each device. Keeps the rack tight and makes tracing connections instant.
3
Keep 6"+ clearance behind and above the NAS for airflow. The DS423+ pulls air through the drives and exhausts out the back — blocking it will raise temperatures and shorten drive life.
4
Run the Mac Mini headless — after initial setup, SSH and VNC mean you never need a monitor plugged into it. Saves a cable and keeps the install tidy.
5
Set static IPs on your router for the Mini and NAS — assign them fixed local IPs (e.g. 192.168.1.10 and 192.168.1.11). Everything stays consistent and you never have to hunt for device addresses.

Project 08 — Package Integrity
Certified Package — Chain of Custody & Theft Claim System
A tamper-evidence and chain-of-custody app for shipping Pokémon cards and collectibles. Photograph and timestamp contents before sealing, track the package through transit, detect tampering or missing contents on arrival, and auto-generate pre-filled claim paperwork for USPS, FedEx, UPS, and others.
Advanced
The Real Problem — Documented Cases
A former USPS sorting clerk in NJ pleaded guilty to stealing $108,692 in Pokémon and sports cards across 10 packages. Mail theft is a federal crime (up to 5 years prison) but victims routinely lose claims because they lack timestamped proof of what was inside before sealing. USPS rejects 30–40% of all insurance claims due to insufficient documentation — this app solves that problem directly.

Build Phases
1
Pre-shipment photo vault
Mobile-first camera UI. Photograph each card, the sealed package, and the label. Every photo is automatically timestamped, GPS-tagged, and stored with a cryptographic hash on your NAS. This is your irrefutable proof of contents before handoff.
2
Package certificate generation
App generates a PDF "Certificate of Package Contents" — a tamper-evident document listing every item, its condition, photos, card values (pulled from your Pokémon price tracker), and a hash fingerprint proving the photos haven't been altered.
3
Live tracking + deadline monitor
Connects to USPS, FedEx, and UPS tracking APIs. Monitors transit in real time. Automatically calculates and displays the claim deadline countdown (60 days) from ship date — sends push alerts at 45, 30, and 7 days remaining.
4
Arrival comparison (AI)
Recipient uses the app to photograph arrival condition. Qwen2.5-VL compares before vs. after images and flags discrepancies — missing cards, resealed packaging, weight differences. Generates a tamper score and flags suspected theft vs. transit damage.
5
Auto-claim form generator
One button generates a complete pre-filled claim package: carrier-specific claim form (USPS/FedEx/UPS), photo evidence bundle, Certificate of Contents, card value documentation from your price tracker, and if theft is suspected — a USPIS postal inspector complaint and police report template.
